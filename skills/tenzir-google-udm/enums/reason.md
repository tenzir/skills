# Reason

Reason for closing an Alert or Case in the SOAR product.

- **Full name**: `google.backstory.Reason`
- **Values**: `4`

## Values

### `REASON_UNSPECIFIED`

- **Number**: `0`

Default reason.

### `REASON_NOT_MALICIOUS`

- **Number**: `1`

Case or Alert not malicious.

### `REASON_MALICIOUS`

- **Number**: `2`

Case or Alert is malicious.

### `REASON_MAINTENANCE`

- **Number**: `3`

Case or Alert is under maintenance.
