# Service.State

The current status of the service.

- **Full name**: `google.backstory.Service.State`
- **Values**: `9`

## Values

### `STATE_UNSPECIFIED`

- **Number**: `0`

Default service status.

### `RUNNING`

- **Number**: `1`

The service is running.

### `STOPPED`

- **Number**: `2`

The service is stopped. This is a Windows-specific service status.

### `PAUSED`

- **Number**: `3`

The service is paused. This is a Windows-specific service status.

### `COMPLETED`

- **Number**: `4`

The service is completed.

### `START_PENDING`

- **Number**: `5`

The service is starting.

### `STOP_PENDING`

- **Number**: `6`

The service is stopping.

### `PAUSE_PENDING`

- **Number**: `7`

The service is pausing.

### `CONTINUE_PENDING`

- **Number**: `8`

The service is continuing.
