# Asset.AssetType

The role type of the asset.

## Values

0. `ROLE_UNSPECIFIED`: Unspecified asset role.
1. `WORKSTATION`: A workstation or desktop.
2. `LAPTOP`: A laptop computer.
3. `IOT`: An IOT asset.
4. `NETWORK_ATTACHED_STORAGE`: A network attached storage device.
5. `PRINTER`: A printer.
6. `SCANNER`: A scanner.
7. `SERVER`: A server.
8. `TAPE_LIBRARY`: A tape library device.
9. `MOBILE`: A mobile device such as a mobile phone or PDA.
