# Relation.Directionality

Describes the relationship model as directed or undirected.

## Values

0. `DIRECTIONALITY_UNSPECIFIED`: Default value.
1. `BIDIRECTIONAL`: Modeled in both directions. Primary entity (a) to related entity (b) and related entity (b) to primary entity (a).
2. `UNIDIRECTIONAL`: Modeled in a single direction. Primary entity (a) to related entity (b).
